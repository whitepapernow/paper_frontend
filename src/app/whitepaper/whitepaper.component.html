# Introduction QmakP2ZAzEEUp ..

The internet has been the catalyst in technological advancement for the last few decades. But it also has been the platform for intellectual thievery and technological mismanagement. We propose a blockchain based platform that builds the best products using mass collaboration and consensus. In the current climate, some workers have been reluctant to perform their best under the assumption that their employer will steal their ideas and they will not be paid fairly. There is a lack of innovation and employees are jumping around from job to job in search of better work benefits and pay. Companies are reaching out for great ideas outside of their employed professionals and at the same time to keep their employees. Companies need a decentralized validation platform in real time to validate their products and work. Workers need a trust platform that rewards them fairly. The world needs only the best products that are well made and use the least resources.

A platform that can show a transparent relationship between product and people, technologically, environmentally, and socioeconomically will essentially be more attractive than an unethical product full of negative externalities. As more products raise the ethics bar, the rest of the products will be held to these standards. This builds a higher standard for the entire economics environment. This builds better products. This uses less resources.

Having the security to give your ideas under time stamped and legal protection will allow people to grow ideas faster. Not only is a worthy idea recognized and rewarded upon implementation, but it also helps evolve other people’s ideas and thus the branches and leaves from the tree of these ideas are rewarded as well. Any time - all or part of these ideas are reused in any smart project, they will be linked and further reward is given. So what may seem like a small reward in the beginning will over time grow as many more smart projects are built on the platform. The gift that keeps on giving is merit.
